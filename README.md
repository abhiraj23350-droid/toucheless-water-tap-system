Touchless Water Tap System is an automatic water dispensing system that works without physical touch.
This project uses a sensor and Arduino to detect the presence of hands and automatically turns the water ON or OFF.
It helps in saving water and maintaining proper hygiene, especially in public places.

ðŸ”¹ Working Principle

The working of the Touchless Water Tap System is very simple:

The IR sensor continuously checks for any object in front of it.

When a hand comes near the sensor, it sends a signal to the Arduino.

Arduino processes the signal and activates the relay module.

The relay turns ON the water pump or solenoid valve.

Water starts flowing automatically.

When the hand is removed, the sensor stops detecting.

Arduino turns OFF the relay, and water flow stops.

Thus, water flows only when needed.

ðŸ”¹ Circuit Explanation

The IR sensor is connected to the Arduino input pin.

The relay module is connected to an output pin of Arduino.

The water pump is controlled using the relay.

Arduino acts as the brain of the system.
